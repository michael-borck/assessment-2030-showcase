Key Innovation: The 'CloudCore' Audit

This assessment features a two-pronged approach to AI integration:

AI as the "Client"

Students interact with AI-powered characters in a simulated business environment

AI as the "Intern"

Students work with LLMs as junior assistants, learning to critically evaluate and refine AI output

Learning Objectives

  • Teach students to think with AI rather than just use AI
  • Develop skills in effective prompting and critical evaluation
  • Prepare students for AI-amplified professional practice
  • Maintain academic integrity through transparency and process assessment
